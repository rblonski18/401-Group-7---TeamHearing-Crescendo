Class: PlayHeadAnimator

PlayHeadAnimator(dom, numBeats, beatCallback, audioCtx)

The playHeadAnimator serves two purposes: 1. Animating the UI playhead to show the user where in the beat is being played 2. Trigger the beat play callback to play beats in time (using a lookahead)
